About Us!

The Shanghai Martial Arts Club - Max Fitness was established in 2013 at Valencia, Mangalore. Our coaches are well-trained in various martial arts disciplines including Taekwondo, Kickboxing, Kung Fu, Wushu, BJJ, and more. From the very beginning, our vision was clear — to empower the youth and children of our district with martial arts skills that enhance both physical and mental strength. Over the years, we have trained children, youth, and adults in multiple combat sports while nurturing confidence, discipline, and character.

In 2015, we grew into a strong community — a family where everyone felt safe, supported, and motivated to become their best selves. By 2017, we began teaching Taekwondo across various schools in and around Mangalore, ensuring quality training at nominal costs so that no student is deprived of learning due to financial limitations. We strongly believe that every student deserves the opportunity to discover their true potential and express it freely.

In 2020, we expanded our journey by forming the M-Tigers (Mangalore Martial Arts) — a mixed martial arts combat team that represented our passion, dedication, and fighting spirit.

Now, in 2025, we take another major step forward with the establishment of the South Zone Martial Arts Foundation India — World of Martial Arts, carrying forward our mission to build a united community of skilled, confident, and empowered martial artists.

Vision

India strives to build a community where people of different backgrounds and abilities can feel secure, respected, and motivated. Mission

The South Zone Martial Arts Foundation India is a martial arts classes for all ages focused on self-actualization, total body healing, and strength. Our goal is to help people tap into their potential, seeking balance and grace through movement, while establishing a strong foundation of integrity and self-discipline that will facilitate personal and professional success.

What We Teach

Our Disciplines

TAEKWONDO
Taekwondo

Olympic sport and Korean martial art famous for its dynamic kicking techniques. We train under ITF and WTF systems with certified black-belt instructors.

BOXING
Boxing

The sweet science of fighting — developing footwork, punching technique, ring intelligence and physical conditioning under professional coaching.

PENCAK SILAT
Pencak Silat

The traditional martial art of Southeast Asia — combining strikes, locks, throws and weapons training. Our students have won at Khelo India Games.

JUMP ROPE
Jump Rope

A competitive sport requiring explosive athleticism and precise coordination. Our instructor Asif Kinya is a certified Jump Rope Referee and Judge.

SILAMBAM
Silambam

Ancient Tamil Nadu staff-fighting art — one of the oldest martial arts in India. Training builds coordination, agility and cultural pride.

YOGA
Yoga

Foundational practice for all martial artists — improving flexibility, balance, mental focus and recovery. Certified yoga instruction for all levels.

WUSHU
Wushu

Chinese martial art combining combat and performance. Training in Sanda (sparring) and Taolu (forms) for competition and personal development.

SELF DEFENCE
Self Defence

Practical self-protection techniques drawn from multiple disciplines — suitable for all ages. Build situational awareness, confidence and safety skills.
